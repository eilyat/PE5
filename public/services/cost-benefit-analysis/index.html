Cost benefit analysis

Do the benefits of your investment exceed the costs?

Making decisions in uncertain times needs precise understanding of the costs and benefits. We can assist by identifying fiscal, economic and wellbeing benefits from your investment decision or policy intervention.

The team at Principal Economics have the experience and credibility to undertake cost-benefit analysis for government and businesses on a wide range of issues. From developing tools and frameworks for Social Cost Benefit Analysis and undertaking Wider Economic Benefit Assessments for public sector clients.

We use our expertise to ensure decision makers are aware of the trade-offs from implementing different strategies and how best to maximise their resources.
